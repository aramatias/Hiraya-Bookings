Frequently Asked Questions

Explore commonly asked questions about booking, event types, services, and support in our FAQs section. Get quick answers to ensure a smooth and informed booking experience at Hiraya Auditorium, Caraga State University's premier event space.

  • How do I make a booking with Hiraya Booking?

    To make a booking, visit our website or mobile app, select your preferred date and space, fill in event details, and proceed with the reservation process.

  • Can I modify or cancel my booking?

    Yes, modifications or cancellations are possible. Please refer to our booking policy for details on terms and conditions.

  • What types of events can I host at Hiraya?

    Hiraya accommodates various events, including conferences, performances, workshops, celebrations, and more. Our spaces cater to diverse event needs.

  • Is technical equipment available at Hiraya for events?

    Yes, we offer technical equipment such as projectors, microphones, and sound systems. These can be selected as add-ons during the booking process.

  • How far in advance should I book my event?

    It's recommended to book as early as possible to secure your preferred date and space. However, last-minute bookings are also accommodated based on availability.
